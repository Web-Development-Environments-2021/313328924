About Me




Amnon Bogin was born in 1938 in the workers' settlement of Kfar Melal, a graduate of the Green Village School.
He served in the army at "Nahal" in the Arava and in 1958 founded Kibbutz Yotvata.
He married a Jewdit grandmother in 1959 and in 1963 they left the kibbutz with thier eldest son Niv, who was born in Yotvata.
Afterwords he was joined "AGED" as driver and later became a member of the board and a member of the Egged Southern Secretariat.
He later served as chairman of the Egged Secretariat and in 1981 set out on an Egged mission to New York to establish a tourism agency to send tourists to Israel.
In 1986 he retired early and has since served as a volunteer in the traffic police.
He is now a full time retiree and enjoys 7 wonderful grandchildren.
